HedgeIQ

Hedge your portfolio at midnight — in 60 seconds.

Executive Briefing · April 2026

Open with the tagline. The whole product is a 60-second answer to a midnight question.

The Problem

"I held 5,000 shares of AAL on the Sunday the Iran-US peace talks collapsed. Oil futures were spiking. I knew I needed protective puts. The Fidelity chain had 240 contracts. It was 11pm. I froze. Markets opened. I lost $2,355."

— HedgeIQ founder, January 2024

This is the founding story. Real money, real night, real freeze. The problem is decision speed under pressure, not knowledge.

Why retail investors don't hedge

  • Cost — premiums feel like waste when nothing happens.
  • Complexity — strikes, expiries, IV, OI, deltas.
  • Time — 240 contracts, 10 hours until open.
  • Jargon — "protective put" is a barrier word.

They have the money. They have the knowledge. They don't have a clear answer at the moment they need one.

The Solution

An AI-powered hedge calculator.

Plain English. Sixty seconds. Three ranked recommendations.

Screenshot: emergency hedge dialog with top 3 puts

Demo Flow

  1. Sign up → connect broker via SnapTrade.
  2. Live positions populate.
  3. Click "Hedge" on any position.
  4. Pick a risk scenario (10% drop / earnings / sector shock).
  5. See top 3 puts ranked by value-score.
  6. Read the AI's plain-English explanation.
  7. Place the order in your broker.

Hedge Algorithm

Deterministic Python. No LLM in the critical path.

  1. Filter chain: PUTs only, expiry 14–90 days, strike 80–105% of spot, OI ≥ 5,000.
  2. For each survivor compute coverage / total_cost at a 10% drop.
  3. Rank by value_score descending. Return top 3.

Full worked AAL example in wiki/07.

AI Layer

  • Model: Claude Haiku — sub-second, sub-cent.
  • Role: translation only. Never the decision.
  • System prompt: clamps behaviour to no specific trade advice.
  • Daily limits: 10 (free), 100 (pro), unlimited (admin).
  • Caching: identical prompts within 1h served from ChromaDB.

Broker Integration

SnapTrade aggregator. Four brokers today: Fidelity, IBKR, Robinhood, Public.

  • Each user gets their own SnapTrade per-user secret on registration.
  • We never reuse the admin secret for end-user calls.
  • Mock fallback when SnapTrade is down — labelled clearly to the user.

Security Posture

  • PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 (100k, salt) — stdlib only.
  • HS256 JWT, 24h expiry, no PII in claims.
  • Strict CSP whitelisting only Anthropic, SnapTrade, Polygon.
  • OWASP headers, GZip, global exception handler (no stack-trace leak).
  • Per-IP and per-user rate limits.
  • SQL injection-safe (ORM only); prompt injection contained by role separation.
